p - put letter at a location
      p <x> <y> <l>

      where (x,y) - location, and
        l - letter in the rack

c - clear the turn

q - exit the Game

r - refresh the console

b - show bonus tile placement

w - input an entire word at location
      w <x> <y> <word> [<v>]

    This inputs a the word at location (x, y).

    Default placement orientation is horizontal.
    "v" is for vertical placement. Optional.
